Functionality of Enhanced Ecommerce Analytics with Magento

How does the Google Enhanced Ecommerce Analytics Extension for Magento work? Following Magento best practices, this light weight module takes responsibility for using layout xml to remove Magentoâ€™s built in Google Analytics ga.js tracking code. The extension inserts properly formatted analytics.js with an include for the 'ec' functionality that google requires for Enhanced Ecommerce Analytics just before the opening body tag. Ecommerce analytics calls have some key considerations, for example once a page view is sent, ecommerce data can not be sent, so we've left the page view send to be responsible for the push to analytics and hooked it just before the closing body tag.

Magento Meets Google's Enhanced Ecommerce Analytics Features

Mediotype's Enhanced Ecommerce Google Analytics Extension provides additional and more advanced features for Google Analytics reporting for Magento platform users. Every manager realizes the importance of collecting timely, useful, and relevant data surrounding their business. With this Google Analytics Extension you will be able to enhance your ability to collect and analyze the data pouring in from your Magento ecommerce site. This will greatly increase your capabilities and understanding of what needs to be done to increase the performance of your website overall in the future.

The ec.js plugin allows you to track a wide range of user shopping experiences and will provide Google Analytics Reports and Enhancement options for re-marketing and demographic reports, including but not limited to:

  • Viewing Product Impressions
  • Product Link Clicks
  • Product Detail Views
  • Added and Removed Items From Shopping Cart
  • When a Checkout Process Has Been Initiated
  • Completed Transactions
  • Data Regarding Promotions

We ensured that the following data is captured as it strictly relates to Magento and Enhanced Ecommerce tracking:

  • Checkout success completely sends all order info to Google Analytics
  • Checkout multi-shipping success completely sends all order info to Google Analytics
  • The analytics.js code is placed right above the closing head tag
  • Display Features are enabled once the anaytics.js has been included
  • Ec.js is included properly with the analytics.js load
  • Page Views are accurate and sent to Google Analytics
  • Product impressions for product page views are accurate and sent to Google Analytics
  • Product impressions for search results are accurate and sent to Google Analytics
  • Product impressions for advanced search results are accurate and sent to Google Analytics
  • Product impressions for category views are accurate and sent to Google Analytics
  • Add-to-cart clicks from the product detail pages are accurately tracked
  • Add-to-cart clicks from the category view pages are accurately tracked
  • Add-to-cart clicks from search pages are accurately tracked
  • Add-to-cart clicks from advanced search pages are accurately tracked

Installation Instructions

Its simple, turn on Magento's Out Of The Box Google Analytics feature, then turn on the Mediotype Google Enhanced Ecommerce feature. Next turn on enhanced ecommerce in your Google Analytics account dashboard

  1. 1 - Turn on Magento's Google Analytics feature, insert the domain's UA code
  2. 2 - Turn on Mediotype's Enhanced Ecommerce Analytics feature
  3. 3 - Turn on Enhanced Ecommerce Analytics in your google analytics account by following these instructions
  4. 4 - Configure your checkout funnel steps to match Magento's checkout steps
